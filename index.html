MY MOTIVATIONS FOR FAC

I have a real passion and love for technology, as well as a drive to improve society. The whole ethos behind FAC is one to better the world, and the Tech for Better programme is super exciting for me, being one of the things I’m looking forward to most on the course.
I think FAC will provide me with a unique, collaborative environment to start my journey in tech. I’ll enjoy learning with like-minded people interested in technology, who are also eager to learn and make a difference.
I believe pair programming is one of the best ways to learn, sharing knowledge with other people and reiterating your own learning is a very effective mode of learning for me. It’s what I have found to work for me during university and work, and I know it will work for me during the course.
I love the mentoring aspect of the course. The community at FAC has been super helpful, and I would love to give back and reinforce my learning through mentoring. The Tuesday sessions have been outstanding for my learning, and I look forward to mentoring and learning simultaneously.
The curriculum of the course is also super enticing for me, I think it will provide me with a wide range of insight and expertise ranging from Node and React to Databases and REST API’s, ultimately allowing me to become a well-rounded web developer.
